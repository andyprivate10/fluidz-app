Pro-exclusive tools

Everything you need for 50 to 500+ guests

These features are added on top of standard Fluidz (profiles, applications, votes, DM, check-in).

Guest dashboard

Full view of all guests. Real-time statuses: invited, confirmed, paid, arrived. Filters, search, CSV export.

Unique QR code

QR generated per guest. Scan at the door. Real-time arrival dashboard with counters.

Time slots

Wave invitations (6:30pm, 7pm, 7:30pm). Max capacity per slot. Guests choose their time.

Digital cloakroom

Digital ticket on scan. Drop-off and pick-up tracked. No more lost paper tickets.

Legal consent

Sign the rules before entering. Time-stamped attestation. Legally compliant.

Staff management

Dedicated roles: reception, cloakroom, security, bar. Mission-based views. Volunteers or participants.

Broadcast

Message all guests or by segment (confirmed, pending, staff). In-app push notifications.

Stats & analytics

Profile distribution (roles, ages). Conversion rates. Full event history.

Template & branding

Reusable event templates. Custom branding (logo, colors). Personalized invitation page.

Zones & areas

Define zones (dance floor, chill, dark room). Capacity per zone. Real-time tracking.

Emergency & security

Instant broadcast alert. Emergency protocol. Discreet reporting. Staff panic button.
